THE STEUART LEGACY

In 1835, brothers James and George Steuart arrived in the island then known as Ceylon to establish George Steuart & Company, which stands proudly today as the oldest establishment in mercantile Sri Lanka.

OUR COMMITMENT TO QUALITY

Recognised fondly as the original Ceylon Tea people, George Steuart Teas (Pvt) Ltd enjoys a leading presence, blending the authenticity of pure Ceylon Tea with technological advancements beyond the contemporary. Steuart Tea is a class of its own in the artistry of tea making. With a vision of bringing to life a century old indulgence to satisfy contemporary beverage needs, the tea produced by George Steuart Teas is at the heights of excellence in quality tea.
